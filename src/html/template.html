Janszen Legacy Project

The Janszen Legacy Project takes place in and throughout the shared, unceded, and asserted territories of many nations, including the pune’luxutth’, quw’utsun’, snuneymuhw, leey’qsun, shts’um’inus, tla'amin, tsawwassen, lək̓ʷəŋən, lhaq'temish and W̱SÁNEĆ peoples, as well as the Indigenous families who have been stewards of these lands and waters since time immemorial. May these legacy biodiversity data be shared in a good way with respect to all who hold rights and responsibilities within the region, for the benefit of present day and future generations.
